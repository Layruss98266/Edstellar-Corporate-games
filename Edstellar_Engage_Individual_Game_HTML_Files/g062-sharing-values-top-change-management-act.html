G062. Sharing Values

Activity/Game 15-30 minutes Individual or small group
Source Context
Top Change Management Activities, Games and Exercises - Change management activities
Concept
Discuss personal-org value alignment
Business Purpose
Strengthen belonging
Skills Developed
Change Readiness
Materials / Setup
Facilitator guide, timer, worksheet or digital board, score/reflection sheet.
How to Play
  1. Introduce the activity goal and connect it to change readiness.
  2. Split participants into the recommended format: individual or small group.
  3. Give the group the core challenge: discuss personal-org value alignment.
  4. Run the activity within the time limit while the facilitator observes teamwork, decision quality, and participation.
  5. Debrief using reflection questions and capture workplace applications or commitments.
Review Questions
  • What did the activity reveal about change readiness in the team?
  • Where did the team lose time, clarity, or alignment?
  • How can the same behavior be applied to real workplace projects?
Key Takeaway
Sharing Values helps employees practice change readiness through a structured workplace activity. The value comes from the debrief: teams should connect what happened in the game to how they plan, communicate, decide, and improve at work.
