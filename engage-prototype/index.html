Anatomy of a session

From setup to debrief in 30 minutes.

A typical Engage session has five beats. Admins schedule and walk away; the platform runs the rest.

01

Schedule

Admin picks a game, invites teams, and sets the date. Two minutes.

02

Walkthrough

Players see an in-game popup explaining concept, rules, and debrief questions.

03

Play

Live or async. Leaderboards, timers, and team rooms run automatically.

04

Debrief

The platform surfaces three structured review questions tied to the game.

05

Reinforce

At 14 and 30 days post-training, the matching game re-runs on autopilot.
