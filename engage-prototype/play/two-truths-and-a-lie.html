How to Play Two Truths and a Lie

Each speaker shares three statements. Two are true. One is a lie. Everyone else votes for the lie.

✍️
1. SubmitEach player privately enters 2 truths and 1 lie.
🎤
2. PresentThe speaker's statements appear in random order.
🗳️
3. VoteEach voter picks the one they think is the lie.
🏆
4. ScoreCorrect voters score. Speakers score when they fool the room.
Good lie

"I once trained for a marathon." Sounds plausible and matches a real workplace persona.

Weak lie

"I have never used Excel." Too obvious — the group catches it instantly.
