How to Play Improv Challenge

Quick workplace scenarios that practice "Yes, and..." thinking, clear communication, fast decisions, and team collaboration.

👥
1. Form teamsUse the demo teams or add 2-6 of your own. Each starts with 50 EngageCoins.
🎯
2. Read the promptEach round shows a workplace scenario with difficulty tag and skill focus.
⚡
3. ImproviseTeams respond using "Yes, and...". Spend coins on power-ups for an edge.
🏆
4. Score & winScore across 6 criteria. Round winner earns +25 bonus coins.

Power-ups (cost in EngageCoins)

Scoring criteria (0-10 each, max 60 per round)

  • Builds - extended on previous idea rather than blocking
  • Clarity - the response was concrete and easy to follow
  • Creativity - novel angle or approach
  • Speed - delivered confidently within the time limit
  • Solution - moved toward an outcome, not just discussion
  • Teamwork - the whole team participated, not just one voice
