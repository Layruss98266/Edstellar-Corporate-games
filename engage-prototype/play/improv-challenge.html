How to Play Improv Challenge

Use quick workplace scenarios to practice “Yes, and...” thinking, clear communication, fast decisions, and team collaboration.

👥
1. Form teams Use the demo teams or add your own teams with 3 to 6 participants each.
🎯
2. Read the prompt Each round gives a workplace scenario such as a client issue, team conflict, or urgent change.
⚡
3. Improvise Teams respond quickly using “Yes, and...” to build on ideas instead of blocking them.
🏆
4. Score live Score clarity, creativity, speed, solution thinking, teamwork, and idea-building. Leaderboard updates instantly.
Example Prompt

“A client changes the requirement after the work is nearly complete. Build the team response.”

Good Response Pattern

“Yes, and we should first clarify the business reason, estimate the impact, then give the client two practical options.”

Weak Response

“No, that will not work.” This blocks the scenario and stops collaboration.

Strong Response

“Yes, and we can involve the client early so they understand the timeline, trade-offs, and next steps.”
