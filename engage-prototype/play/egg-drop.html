How to Play Egg Drop Challenge Pro

Drag materials onto the egg, manage cost and weight, run drop tests across 3 escalating heights.

👥
1. Form teamsAdd teams or use demo teams. Each gets 100 budget.
🧰
2. BuildDrag materials onto the egg. Placement matters — cushion at bottom, drag on top.
🌪️
3. Handle eventRandom events change cost, drag, or difficulty mid-round.
🏆
4. Drop & reviewRun drop test. Auto-review explains every choice.
Good design

Cotton/sponge at bottom (impact), straws/cardboard around the core (structure), balloon/bag on top (drag).

Weak design

All budget on heavy structure with no cushioning usually cracks. Heavy ≠ safer.
